The Arista 7150 series uses Barefoot/Intel Fulcrum silicon (FM6000), optimized for ultra-low latency.

| Model | Ports | Total Buffer |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| DCS-7150S-24 | 24 SFP+ | 9.5 MB |
| DCS-7150S-52 | 52 SFP+ | 9.5 MB |
| DCS-7150S-64 | 48 SFP+ and 4 QSFP | 9.5 MB |

TX buffer details are not published for this series.
