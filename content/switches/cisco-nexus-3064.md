The Cisco Nexus 3064X is built on the **Broadcom Trident+** (BCM56840) ASIC.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 48 SFP+ and 4 QSFP |
| RX Queues | 12Q |
| Total Buffer | 9 MB |
| TX Buffer (max to 1 port) | 5 MB |
