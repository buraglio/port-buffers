The Dell 8132F and 4032F are built on the **Broadcom Trident+** (BCM56840) ASIC.

| Model | Ports | RX Queues | Total Buffer |
|-------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 8132F | 24 SFP+ and 2x QSFP | 8Q | 9 MB |
| 4032F | 24 SFP+ and 2x QSFP | 8Q | 9 MB |

TX buffer data not available from Dell's data sheet.
