The Pica8 P-3920 is a white-box switch built on the **Broadcom Trident+** (BCM56840) ASIC, running PicOS (OpenFlow capable).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 48 SFP+ and 4x QSFP |
| Total Buffer | 9 MB |
