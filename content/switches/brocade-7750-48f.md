The Brocade VDX 7750-48F is built on the **Broadcom Trident II** ASIC.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 48 SFP+ and 6 QSFP and expansion |
| RX Queues | 8Q |
| Total Buffer | 12.2 MB |
| TX Buffer (max to 1 port) | Dynamic up to 8 MB |
