The IBM G8264 is built on the **Broadcom Trident+** (BCM56840) ASIC.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 48 SFP+ and 4 QSFP |
| RX Queues | 8Q |
| Total Buffer | 9 MB/switch |
| TX Buffer | Not on data sheet |

The TX buffer size was not published by IBM. A Tolly Group report performed microburst testing on this switch — see the [Tolly report](/assets/pdfs/tolly-IBM-G8264.PDF).
