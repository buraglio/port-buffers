The Juniper QFX5100-24Q is built on the **Broadcom Trident II** ASIC.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 24 QSFP + expansion slots |
| RX Queues | 8Q |
| Total Buffer | 12 MB |
| TX Buffer (max to 1 port) | Dynamic up to 8 MB |
