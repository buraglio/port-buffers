The Allied Telesis DC2552 is built on the **Broadcom Trident+** (BCM56840) ASIC.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Ports | 48 SFP+ and 4 QSFP |
| RX Queues | 8Q |
| Total Buffer | 9 MB |
| TX Buffer | 5 MB |
